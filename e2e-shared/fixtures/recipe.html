Pistachio-Crusted Salmon (E2E Fixture)

A deterministic recipe fixture used by the Mini Mealie E2E suite.

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • 1/2 cup shelled pistachios, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (200C) and line a baking sheet.
  2. Whisk mustard and honey; brush over the salmon.
  3. Press chopped pistachios onto the tops of the fillets.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake 12-15 minutes until the salmon flakes easily.
